Hello!

I'm just a person who loves making things work.

I've been lucky to work on everything from pure web apps to data center networking software,
and in my current role I have had the ability to pick the tooling and architecture for our front end.
Such a big challenge is gratifying, humbling, and I love every minute of it.

While I'm always game for learning something new, these technologies are my home turf.

I began building things with JavaScript, Git, Ruby, Rails, HTML, and CSS. Over time I have added Vue.js (+ Vuex, Vue-Router), Axios, Webpack, Python, Django, C#, React, Redux, SQL (SSMS, postgreSQL, MySQL, SQLite) and some others to my toolkit. My current task is building a SPA on top of Django API endpoints, written in Vue.js. I also have had the opportunity to work with Model-View-Template, Model-View-ViewModel, and Model-View-Controller paradigms.
